A Bit About Me

A web developer who loves to create painless user experience. I will graduate from UNC Charlotte’s full-stack web development bootcamp in early May. My strength lies in my adaptability as well as my understanding of constant and efficient communication between team members. Two things ingrained within me from years of Soccer.

I learned many of my skills and values through sport. I was a two time Academic All-American for Wingate University and played for Semi-pro team, the Myrtle Beach Mutiny. I stay involved by doing commentary for Wingate's Soccer Teams on ESPN3.
